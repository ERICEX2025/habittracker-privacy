Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 3, 2026

Habit Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal habit tracking application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

When you use Habit Tracker, we collect and store the following data:

  • Account information: Your name and email address, provided through Sign in with Apple. Apple may hide your real email address if you choose that option.
  • Habit data: Categories, habits, and daily completion records you create within the app.
  • Journal entries: Daily mood selections and notes you optionally provide.
  • Profile photo: An optional profile picture you upload from your camera or photo library.
  • App settings: Your preferences such as theme, notification time, and display settings.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide the app's functionality:

  • Display your habits and track your daily progress
  • Send optional daily reminder notifications at your chosen time
  • Sync your data across your devices

We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising or analytics.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase, which uses Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure. All data is protected by row-level security, meaning only you can access your own data. Data is transmitted over HTTPS encryption.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple Sign In: Used for authentication. We receive your name and email (or a relay address) from Apple. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Used for data storage and authentication. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Expo: Used for push notifications delivery. See Expo's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as you have an active account. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon account deletion, all your data is permanently removed from our servers.

Children's Privacy

Habit Tracker is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
